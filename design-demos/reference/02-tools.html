Part 2 · The Tool Landscape

Every major assistant has an Ask mode: you converse, it answers.

Claude

Strong with long documents and careful reasoning.

ChatGPT

The most widely used; broadest feature set.

Gemini

Lives inside Google Workspace; reads very long context.

Microsoft Copilot

Built into Word, Excel, Outlook, and Teams.

Same underlying idea — a chat window over a large language model. Your choice usually follows where your documents already live.
