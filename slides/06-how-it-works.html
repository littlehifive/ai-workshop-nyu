Token

The unit of text the model reads and writes, roughly a fragment of a word.

Model

The trained statistical system that produces these probabilities.

Context window

How much text it can “see” at once when it predicts.

There is no database lookup behind the answer and no guarantee of understanding. The model produces a well-calibrated guess from patterns in its training data.
