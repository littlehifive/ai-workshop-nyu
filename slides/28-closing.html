A last idea to take home: System 3

Wharton researchers Steven Shaw and Gideon Nave argue that AI now forms a third system of thinking alongside Kahneman's fast intuition and slow deliberation. Offloading a task to AI, the way you hand arithmetic to a calculator, keeps your reasoning in control. Cognitive surrender is different: the AI makes the decision and you adopt it as your own without noticing the transfer. In their experiments, people's confidence rose whether the AI was right or wrong.
