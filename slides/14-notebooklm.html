NotebookLM: a librarian that only cites your shelf

NotebookLM

What it does

Upload up to 50 sources on the free tier: PDFs, Docs, links, and audio. When you ask a question, every answer cites the passages it drew from, and you can click each one to verify it.

Studio extras

Audio Overviews (podcast-style discussion of your sources), mind maps, and flashcards.

Why researchers care

It is a closed system: it answers from the documents you give it, not from the open internet. This is what grounding looks like as a finished product.
