Activity 1 · 10 Min

Prompt makeover

See how much of an answer's quality comes from the quality of your request.

  1. 1Open an AI tool you already know (ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, or Copilot)
  2. 2Write a quick, minimal version of a real request from your work, and run it
  3. 3Rewrite it with four elements: a role for the AI to assume, the context that matters, the structure you want, and constraints on quality
  4. 4Run it again and compare the two outputs side by side
