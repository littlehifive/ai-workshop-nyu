Part 4 · Takeaways

Three takeaways to carry out of this room

1

The tools move fast

The landscape changes in weeks: what is popular and capable today can be obsolete soon, which makes choosing and verifying tools genuinely confusing. Following every update is almost a full-time job. Stay aware of how rapidly this technology is reshaping your research and your practice.

2

Evaluate it consistently

Judge AI with a repeatable method, both when you build or assess a tool and for your own day-to-day use. The four-level framework gives you the rungs: model, product, user, and impact.

3

Mind your agency

There are very non-agentic and very agentic ways to use AI. It is genuinely powerful, so stay mindful of how your personal, proxy, and collective agency shifts in relation to it.
