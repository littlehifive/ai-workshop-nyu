Part 4 · Takeaways

Three caveats to carry out of this room

1

Tooling

Products change often, but the underlying capabilities (ask, do, remember) stay stable.
Choose by where your documents live, and re-evaluate quarterly at most.

2

Quality

“Seems fine” is not evidence.
Keep the test questions you red-teamed with today, and run them again whenever a tool ships a new model.

3

Agency

AI produces the draft, and you make the decisions.
Once you stop verifying its output, the work is no longer research.
