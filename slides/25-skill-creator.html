Activity 4 · Optional · 15 Min

Teach Claude how you work

Turn a task you repeat into a reusable skill, so Claude reproduces your method instead of starting from scratch each time. This is the deepest level of “remember” from earlier.

  1. 1Pick a task you do the same way often: formatting a report, screening abstracts, drafting a standard reply.
  2. 2In Claude, start the skill-creator with /skill-creator and describe the task in plain language.
  3. 3Answer its questions. It scaffolds the skill for you, writing the instructions and bundling any resources.
  4. 4Run the skill on a real example, then refine the wording until the output matches how you would do it.

What you walk away with

A reusable skill that captures your process, that Claude applies on demand and that you can share with colleagues.

Before you start

Skills run in the Claude desktop app on a paid plan (Pro, Max, Team, or Enterprise). They are not available on the free tier.
