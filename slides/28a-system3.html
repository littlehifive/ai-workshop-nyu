AI is becoming a third system of thinking

System 1Fast and automatic
Intuition. Milliseconds, no effort.
System 2Slow and deliberate
Reasoning. Seconds to minutes, high effort.
System 3AI-mediated
The model thinks; you receive. Zero effort on your side.

Cognitive offloading (healthy)

Your reasoning stays engaged

You System 3
assists System 2
checks Your
answer

Your reasoning stays in the loop.

Cognitive surrender (the risk)

Your reasoning is bypassed

You System 3
decides System 2
checks Your
answer

The decision transfers without you noticing.
