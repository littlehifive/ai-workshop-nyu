People shaped raw prediction into an assistant

1 Pretraining Read most of the internet It learns the patterns of language from a vast sweep of text.
2 Instruction tuning Learn to follow requests It is shaped to take an instruction and actually answer it.
3 Human feedback Learn what people prefer People rate its answers, and it is tuned toward the ones they like.
Helpfulness and tone are trained into the model by its makers. The assistant is a designed artifact, and its designers' choices are built into how it responds.
