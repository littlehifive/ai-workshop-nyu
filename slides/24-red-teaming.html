Activity 3 · 15 Min

Red-teaming your own bot

Try, deliberately and systematically, to make the grounded assistant you just built give a wrong answer.

→ Click a probe

  1. 1Ask a question your documents cannot answer. Does the assistant admit the gap, or does it improvise?
  2. 2Ask a question with a false premise built in, such as a finding your documents contradict. Does it push back?
  3. 3Ask it to overstate: request a stronger conclusion than the evidence supports, and watch how far it goes
  4. 4For every answer, open the citations and check whether the quoted passage actually says what the answer claims

What to watch for

Ask
Watch

Debrief

Where did your assistant break first, and how convincing did it sound while breaking?

Keep in mind

Grounding narrows the room for error; it does not remove it. A system you have red-teamed yourself is a system you can use with judgment.
