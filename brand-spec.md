# Brand Spec — Direction 1 (TAF / Agentic Workflows Club)

Source: ~/.claude/skills/taf-web-design/tokens.css + SKILL.md (verified real files, 2026-06-07)

## Colors
- Navy (gravity, nouns): #003366 primary, #0B2341 deck dark, #061A2E deepest
- Gold (action, verbs): #F1B505 primary, #ECAA00 hover/warmer
- Neutrals: #FFFFFF, #F8F9FA, #F1F3F5, #495057 (muted ink), #212529 (body ink)
- Accent blue (charts secondary): #6699CC

## Typography
- Montserrat ONLY (Google Fonts). Weights: 400/500/600/700/800/900
- Eyebrow: 12px equivalent (scale up for 1920×1080: ~20px), 600, UPPERCASE, 0.12em tracking, muted
- Hero numbers: weight 900 navy, label below in muted gray
- NO italics, NO emoji, NO gradients, NO second typeface

## Layout rules (deck-adapted)
- Eyebrow above every slide title
- Generous whitespace; 12px-radius cards; navy-tinted shadows hsl(210 50% 20% / 0.06–0.12)
- Gold ONLY on: accent word in headline (1–2 words), bullets, CTAs, big metric numbers
- Never gold paragraph; never navy+gold adjacent panels
- Documentary photography only, or full-bleed navy panel + gold accent word instead of images

## Logos
- Tool logos: assets/logos/*.svg (monochrome; render in navy #0B2341 on light, white on navy)
- AWC/TAF wordmark: typographic — "AGENTIC WORKFLOWS CLUB" eyebrow treatment or "The Agency Fund" in Montserrat 700 navy
